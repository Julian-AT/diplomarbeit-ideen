# Plan 01-02: Characterize Content, Language, Metadata, and Parsing/OCR Needs

<objective>
Characterize the unpacked corpus for text yield, visual density, language signals, recurring document types, and OCR/parsing needs.
</objective>

<tasks>
<task id="01-02-T1" type="implementation">
<read_first>
- `.planning/corpus/manifest.json`
- `scripts/corpus_discovery.py`
</read_first>
<action>
Sample PDFs with PyMuPDF when available, recording page count, sampled text characters per page, sampled image count per page, low-text/OCR candidate flag, language guess, and bounded excerpts. Extract text snippets from `.docx` and `.pptx` OpenXML files using standard ZIP/XML parsing.
</action>
<acceptance_criteria>
- `.planning/corpus/content-samples.json` contains `pdf_samples`.
- Each PDF sample records `text_yield`, `ocr_candidate`, and `language_guess`.
- `.planning/corpus/content-samples.json` contains `office_samples` for `.docx` and `.pptx` files.
</acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task id="01-02-T2" type="implementation">
<read_first>
- `.planning/corpus/content-samples.json`
- `.planning/corpus/inventory-summary.json`
</read_first>
<action>
Probe local video metadata with `ffprobe` when available and record duration/resolution evidence. Treat video content as metadata-only unless a transcript or user-approved transcription path exists.
</action>
<acceptance_criteria>
- `.planning/corpus/content-samples.json` contains `video_samples`.
- Video samples include `duration_seconds`, `width`, and `height` when `ffprobe` can read the file.
- Reported design does not assume video transcription is available.
</acceptance_criteria>
</task>
</tasks>

<verification>
Inspect `.planning/corpus/content-samples.json` and confirm PDF, Office, and video sections are present with bounded excerpts only.
</verification>

<success_criteria>

</success_criteria>
